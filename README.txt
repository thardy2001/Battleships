Use the following to compile the program:
'javac Battleship.java'

To execute the program:
'java Battleship x y'
where,
x: the broadcast address
y: the broadcast port

When a game begins, both players are presented with their board. The ships are already randomly placed.

When prompted to take your turn, players enter their targeted coordinates in the following format: 

'ij'
Where i is a letter between a and j (not case sensitive) , and j is a number between 1 and 10.

Notice there is no space seperating the letter coordinate and the number coordinate.
